Who We Are

INKINGI CREATIVE HUB is a vibrant community dedicated to empowering youth through art, creativity, and storytelling. We believe every young person has hidden potential waiting to be unlocked through creative expression.

Our Mission

To create a safe space where youth can explore their creativity, build confidence, and develop skills that will shape their future. Through art and community, we foster positive change and personal growth.

Our Vision

A Rwanda where every young person has access to creative opportunities, where art drives social change, and where communities thrive through cultural expression and mutual support.
